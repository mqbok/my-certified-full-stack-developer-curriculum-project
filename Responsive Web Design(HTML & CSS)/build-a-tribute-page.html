NIKOLA TESLA

A genius inventor who revolutionized electricity.

Basic Info

  • Full Name: Nikola Tesla
  • Birth: July 10, 1856
  • Death: January 7, 1943
  • Nationality: Serbian-American
  • Occupation: Inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, physicist

Major Contributions

  • Alternating Current (AC) – Revolutionized power distribution
  • Tesla Coil – High-voltage, high-frequency transformer still used today
  • Wireless Communication – Early work that paved the way for radio
  • Induction Motor – Key to modern appliances and machines

Legacy

  • Over 300 patents
  • Contributions often overlooked during his lifetime, but now widely celebrated
  • The unit of magnetic flux density is named the tesla (T) in his honor
  • Inspired generations of scientists and engineers

Interesting Facts

  • Had a photographic memory
  • Slept only 2 hours a day
  • Spoke 8 languages
  • Claimed to have received signals from extraterrestrials
