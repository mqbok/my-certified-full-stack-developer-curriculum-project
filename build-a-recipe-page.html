Chocolate Cake Recipe

Follow this simple recipe to create delicious chocolate cake.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan with butter or nonstick spray.
  2. Mix ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Pour the batter into the greased pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  4. Cool the cake, then frost with chocolate icing or enjoy as-is!
